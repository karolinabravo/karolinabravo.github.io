About me

Data, tourism & digital content in one profile

I’m Karolina Bravo, a multidisciplinary profile combining tourism & hospitality analytics with digital content creation for beauty and lifestyle brands.

Who I am

I specialize in transforming complex data into actionable insights, especially around tourism and hospitality in Barcelona, while also creating UGC and digital content for brands in beauty, skincare, perfumes and lifestyle.

My work sits at the intersection of analytics, creativity and strategy: from forecasting hotel occupancy and analyzing tourism flows to planning content that connects with real people on TikTok, Instagram and YouTube.

What I do

  • Data analysis, forecasting and BI dashboards (Python, Power BI, GitHub, Colab)
  • Tourism & hospitality analytics using AENA, INE, Dataestur, Frontur, Egatur
  • UGC content for beauty, skincare, perfumes, lifestyle and fashion
  • Digital storytelling, content strategy and personal branding

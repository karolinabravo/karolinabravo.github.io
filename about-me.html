About me

I’m Karolina Bravo, a multidisciplinary profile combining tourism & hospitality analytics with digital content creation for beauty and lifestyle brands.

I specialize in transforming complex data into actionable insights, especially around tourism behaviour and hotel performance in Barcelona. At the same time, I create UGC content for brands in beauty, skincare, perfumes and lifestyle, designing pieces that feel real and are aligned with each brand’s identity.

My work sits at the intersection of analytics, creativity and strategy: from forecasting hotel occupancy with open data sources to planning TikTok and Instagram content that connects with real people.

What I do

  • Data analysis, forecasting & BI dashboards (Python, Power BI, GitHub, Colab)
  • Tourism & hospitality analytics using AENA, INE, Dataestur, Frontur, Egatur
  • UGC content for beauty, skincare, perfumes, lifestyle & fashion
  • Digital storytelling, content strategy & personal branding
